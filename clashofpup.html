Clash Of Puppets

 

 

 

OVERVIEW:

The title is an Hack’n’Slash Platformer featuring funny b-movie characters.

 

Gallery:



Features:

High quality 3d Graphics Fully animated 3D characters with dynamic accurate lighting.

  • Funny to Play Beat nice enemies with ludicrous voices inspired to classical b-movies.
  • Vast Arsenal Find and use tons of devastating weapons and traps.
  • Long Lasting 30 levels in 3 different worlds, plus an infinite Survival Mode.
  • Challenge your Friends try to achieve the best score to be the Survival Hero.
  • Console-Style Experience a true console Hack’n’Slash Platformer game on your mobile device.
  • Gamecenter Leaderboards support and lots of achievements.
