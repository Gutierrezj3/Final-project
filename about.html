Established in 2010 by Domenico Gallo and Manlio Greco, Drakkar Dev is a game development company based in Catania, Italy. Our core mission is to create engaging video games across various platforms, including home consoles, PC, and mobile devices. Leveraging third-party game engines, we strive to deliver high-quality experiences efficiently. With a diverse portfolio spanning genres and platforms, we prioritize creativity and player satisfaction. Drakkar Dev remains committed to pushing the boundaries of gaming while fostering a culture of collaboration and innovation.
