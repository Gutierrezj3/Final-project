Monkey Racing

 

From Chimp to Champ!

 

 

______________________________________________________________________________________

 

Overview:

The title is an arcade karting game featuring crazy monkey characters struggling for victory riding ludicrous vehicles.



 

Features:

  • 80 tracks
  • unlockable customizable vehicles in more than 40 millions of combinations
  • 18 bosses to beat
  • Supported languages: English, Italian, French
  • Single and Multiplayer mode
  • 89 rewarding achievements to unlock
  • improve your vehicle stats and make it faster
  • use any means to win: including missiles, shockwaves and shields
